About

Li Lingna is a PhD researcher in Sociology of Education at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (Malaysia). She obtained her Master's degree in Curriculum and Instruction (English) from Northwest Normal University.

Before pursuing doctoral studies, she worked in the Qingyang Municipal Education System in China, where she taught English at primary school, middle school, and high school levels. Her career reflects extensive experience across the entire spectrum of basic education, particularly in rural educational contexts.

She holds the TEM-8 certificate for English majors.
